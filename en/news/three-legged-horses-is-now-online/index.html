"Three-legged horses" is now online

22.07.2013

One of the pearls of last year's festival program is now available online.

The short film "Three-legged horses" by Felipe Bustos Sierra - one of the pearls of last year's festival program - is now available online. It's about a cash-strapped rickshaw driver who agrees despite his bad knees to take three drunks on a pointless journey all the way to the Edinburgh Castle. The short film is based on a true story and was screened at 35 festivals in 18 countries. By the way: the production costs were covered entirely by crowd funding - as was this year's festival trailer.
