Emmentaler Filmtage 2013: Already over...

Already over and yet still so close...

The 4th edition of the Emmentaler Filmtage tempted 800 visitors from all over to come to the Rüttihubelbad Arts Center and filled our screening rooms to the brim. Many films started deep conversations... but not only the films. Our attempts to create a sustainable and fair-minded festival concept bear fruit and registered in the minds of the experts, the visitors and the media like never before. 

The collection amounts to CHF 70.- for each filmmaker – a new record!
