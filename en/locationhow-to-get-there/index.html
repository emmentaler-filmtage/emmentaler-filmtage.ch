Location/How go get there

   

Location

The Emmentaler Filmtage will take place at the Rüttihubelbad in Walkringen, a social, cultural and conference centre amidst the enchanting scenery of the Emmental. It is situated on a hill above Worb only 12km from Berne and it is easily accessible by car and public transport. Nursing home, socio-therapeutic community, hotel & restaurant, Sensorium & educational centre make the Rüttihubelbad a meeting place. The arts centre has a well established name over a wide region.

With its friendly atmosphere the Rüttihubelbad has the ideal ambience for the Emmentaler Filmtage. The idyllic site and the breathtaking scenery add a pinch of holiday feeling. The restaurant serves a refined local and organic cuisine. You are invited to relax at the lounge and leisurely reflect on your film experience.
The Cafeteria with shop and our snack bar make sure you won't go hungry in between or during the screenings.

The Emmentaler Filmtage will transform the exceptional facilities of the Rüttihubelbad into projection rooms full of atmosphere. Enjoy watching films in an old vaulted cellar, a architecturally striking pavilion, a library and an underground spring.

Travel by car and public transport:

The Rüttihubelbad arts centre is easily accessible by public transport. There are good connections from Thun, Berne & Burgdorf with a bus stop directly in front of the Rüttihubelbad.

Shuttle service

The last postal car with connection to the train departs at 8 pm at the Rüttihubelbad. In addition we offer a free shuttle service after 8 pm at Friday and Saturday evening. The shuttle bus is only running on request. Please contact us at the information stand.
